Not just another business card.

Intro

Holistic Kiwi is a massage & skincare company located in Delft, NL.
It was time to renew their buesiness cards.
We challenged them, and asked what they wanted do achieve with the business cards.
We also discovered people dont really use business cards.
This created an opportunity to rethink what we wanted to achieve with this touchpoint.

The design problem.

Acquisition cost

The current affiliate service that was used had a 60 euro cost for first time clients. Resulting in the first time client not generating any income for the business.

Retention quality

The quality of clients coming in through affiliates only had a 20% retention of becoming a long time customer, combined with the high cost, made this a huge waste of resources.

Desposability of cards

Every business needs business cards, but people often throw them away, lose them, or dont really do anything with them. We wanted a way for the business cards to retain value as a touchpoint.

Defining success.

First time customer €20

Reducing the first time client cost by €40 would give the business some freedom to reward loyal customers.

80% rebooking retention

Having a higher rebooking retention would allow the business to grow faster without having to depend on costly marketing stratagies.

Solution concept.

A referal gift card

The giver & the reciever both recieve a €10 discount, this way they both benefit and turn every happy client into a marketing touchpoint.

Leverage social enginering

A €10 euro discount is nice, but getting it from a trusted friend gives it more value to try out.

Would you throw away €10?

Sustainability & saving costs often go hand in hand. Having clients redistributing the cards, gave them a long lasting touchpoint.

Visual design breakdown.

Bankcard sized

What’s another card in your wallet? having he card fit in your wallet makes it so you are mostly always carrying it around ready to give to that friend that needs a well deserved massage.

Magnet strip = Value

People associate cards with a magnet strip as valuable, after all, al your valuable cards have them (bank/credit cards)
